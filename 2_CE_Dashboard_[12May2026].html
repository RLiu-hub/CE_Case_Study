Median ROA by CE tercile × horizon
Within each horizon window, firms split into low/mid/high CE terciles. Low-CE firms post negative ROA at every horizon.
How to read this: "Horizon" = how many consecutive years of CE data a firm has in the panel. 1-yr covers all 365 firm-years; 5-yr covers only the 73 firms present in every year 2020–2024. The consistent upward trend in median PM (+31%), ROE (+50%) and ROA (+42%) from 1-yr to 5-yr horizon shows that sustained CE engagement is associated with stronger financial performance — not just CE in any single year. The CE–ROA correlation stays positive and statistically significant across 1–4 year windows (r = 0.27 → 0.18, all p < 0.05), narrowing as n shrinks at the 5-yr window. Within each horizon, low-CE firms consistently post negative or near-zero median ROA while mid/high-CE firms remain positive.
